# Customer Behavior Analysis

## Goal
Analyze customer purchase patterns to identify top customers and behavior trends.

## Tools
- Python (Pandas)
- SQL
- Excel / Power BI

## Steps
1. Clean customer orders data
2. Aggregate orders and total spend per customer
3. Identify top 10% customers
4. Create charts and dashboards for visualization

## Outcome
Generated insights on customer purchasing patterns and dashboards for business decision-making.
